Betting Rules

General Rules

  1. All betting is done with play money only - no real money is involved.
  2. Each user starts with $10,000 in play money per season.
  3. Users must be authenticated via Discord to participate.
  4. One account per person - duplicate accounts will be banned.

Betting Rules

  1. Minimum bet amount is $1.
  2. Maximum bet amount is your current balance.
  3. Bets must be placed before the game starts.
  4. Once placed, bets cannot be cancelled or modified.
  5. Only one bet per game per user is allowed.

Payouts

  1. Winning bets pay out at 2:1 (double your money).
  2. Losing bets forfeit the entire wager amount.
  3. In case of a tie game, bets are returned (push).
  4. Payouts are processed automatically after game completion.

Fair Play

  1. No exploiting bugs or glitches - report them instead.
  2. No harassment or abuse of other players.
  3. Decisions by administrators are final.
  4. Have fun and bet responsibly!
