Betting Rules & Guidelines

Everything you need to know about fair play, betting limits, and having fun in our Discord community!

General Rules

1

All betting is done with play money only - absolutely no real money is involved.

2

Each user starts with $10,000 in virtual currency at the beginning of each season.

3

Users must be authenticated via Discord to participate in the platform.

4

One account per person - duplicate accounts will be banned to ensure fair play.

Betting Guidelines

1

Minimum bet amount is $1 per wager.

2

Maximum bet amount is your current balance (all-in allowed!).

3

Bets must be placed 5 minutes before game start - no late bets!

4

Once placed, bets cannot be cancelled or modified - choose wisely!

5

One bet per game per user - make it count!

Payout Structure

Winning Bets

2:1

Double your money! Bet $100, win $200 total.

Losing Bets

0:1

Forfeit the entire wager amount. Better luck next time!

Tie Games

Push

Your wager is returned. No win, no loss.

💡 Pro Tip: Payouts are processed automatically after game completion using official NFL results!
