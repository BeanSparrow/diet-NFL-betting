About Diet NFL Betting

What is Diet NFL Betting?

Diet NFL Betting is a play-money betting platform designed for Discord communities who want to enjoy the excitement of NFL betting without any real financial risk.

Our platform integrates seamlessly with Discord, allowing community members to compete against each other throughout the NFL season using virtual currency.

Key Features

  • Discord Integration: Login with your Discord account
  • Play Money System: Start with $10,000 virtual currency
  • Simple Betting: Pick winners, no complex spreads
  • Live Updates: Real-time game scores and results
  • Community Competition: Leaderboards and statistics
  • Safe & Legal: No real money gambling involved

How We Get Game Data

We use publicly available NFL game data to ensure accurate and timely information for all games, scores, and results. Bets are automatically settled based on official game outcomes.

Disclaimer

This is NOT real money gambling. Diet NFL Betting is purely for entertainment purposes within Discord communities. No real money can be deposited, wagered, or withdrawn. This platform is designed for fun competition among friends and community members.
